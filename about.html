BuildMeal was born from a simple idea: great food shouldn't be a guessing game. We believe every person deserves a meal crafted exactly to their taste — not a one-size-fits-all product off an assembly line.


Founded in 2019 by two food-obsessed developers in a small kitchen, we've grown from a local delivery experiment into a full-scale smart restaurant system. Our menu is crafted fresh daily using locally-sourced ingredients, and every single order is built from scratch, on demand.
