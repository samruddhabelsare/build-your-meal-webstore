FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

How long does delivery take? +
Our average delivery time is 28 minutes. During peak hours (12–2pm, 6–9pm) it may take up to 45 minutes. You'll receive a real-time update once your order is dispatched.
Can I modify my order after placing it? +
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 5 minutes of placement. After that, the kitchen has already started preparing your food. Contact us immediately via phone if you need to make changes.
Do you cater for allergies? +
Yes! Please specify your dietary requirements and allergies in the message field when contacting us. Our kitchen team is trained to handle common allergens. We currently cater to gluten-free, nut-free, and dairy-free diets on request.
